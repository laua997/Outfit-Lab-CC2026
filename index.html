Upload a full-body photo and clothing images, pick pieces, and run virtual try-on (IDM-VTON via the ITP/IMA Replicate proxy). Your closet syncs per Google account in Firebase.

Sign in to save your closet and run try-on.

Why sign in?

Firebase stores your body photo, garment images, and closet metadata under your account. Try-on calls go from your browser to the ITP proxy (no personal Replicate billing required for the proxy path).

2. Add a clothing photo

3. Pick an outfit
